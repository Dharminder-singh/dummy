About US
Welcome To The SIMM

Welcome to The SIMM (School of Innovative Medical Marketing) — a leading institute dedicated to shaping the future of aspiring Medical Representatives. Our mission is to bridge the gap between education and industry by providing world-class training that equips individuals with the knowledge, confidence, and professional skills needed to succeed in the pharmaceutical sector.

At SIMM, we offer a comprehensive 100-hour interactive training program designed to deliver in-depth insights into the pharmacy and healthcare industries. Our curriculum covers essential topics such as pharmacology, pharmaceutical marketing, sales techniques, communication skills, and ethical practices — all delivered through engaging lectures, practical workshops, and real-world case studies.
